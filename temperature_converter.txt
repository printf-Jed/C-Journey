### C Concepts Used:
1. **stdio.h** – Includes input/output functions like printf and scanf.
2. **Functions** – convertTemperature() and printConversionTable() to improve modularity.
3. **Conditional Statements (if-else)** – To determine the conversion type.
4. **Loops (for loop)** – Used in tabular format to iterate over values.
5. **Formatted Output (printf)** – Displays the table with proper alignment.

### Temperature Conversion Formulae:
1. **C to F:** F = (C × 9/5) + 32
2. **C to K:** K = C + 273.15
3. **F to C:** C = (F - 32) × 5/9
4. **F to K:** K = (F - 32) × 5/9 + 273.15
5. **K to C:** C = K - 273.15
6. **K to F:** F = (K - 273.15) × 9/5 + 32
